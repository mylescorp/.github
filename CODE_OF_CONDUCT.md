# MylesCorp Technologies — Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

At MylesCorp Technologies, we are committed to building a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community for everyone — regardless of age, background, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to make participation in our projects and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

**Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and our mission
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Celebrating East African innovation and diverse perspectives

**Examples of unacceptable behaviour:**

- The use of sexualised language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behaviour and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behaviour.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other MylesCorp Technologies community spaces — and also applies when an individual is representing MylesCorp Technologies in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the MylesCorp Technologies team at:

📧 **[info@mylescorptech.com](mailto:info@mylescorptech.com)**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by the leadership of MylesCorp Technologies.
